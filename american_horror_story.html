American Horror Story

Description

American Horror Story is a horror television series created by Ryan Murphy and Brad Falchuk. Each season tells an independent story with a different cast of characters, but some actors return in different roles in later seasons. The series is known for its dark atmosphere, unexpected twists, and disturbing themes that explore horror in its various forms. Each season has its own title and unique setting, making it a unique and terrifying experience for viewers.
